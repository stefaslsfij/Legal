How We Ensure Legal Compliance

At Spectrax Lab, we ensure our services are legally compliant in Qatar by adhering to the following key regulations:

  • Qatar’s Cybercrime Law (Law No. 14 of 2014): This law regulates cyber activities and criminalizes unauthorized access to systems, data theft, and illegal hacking. We ensure all our hacking services are authorized and conducted ethically.
  • Qatar’s Data Protection Law: Our services comply with Qatar's data protection regulations to ensure that no personal or sensitive information is unlawfully accessed or shared. We ensure all data handling is done with the utmost care and privacy.
  • Penetration Testing Authorization: Our pentesting services are only performed after obtaining explicit consent from the involved parties. We follow international ethical hacking guidelines to ensure no harm is caused during testing.
  • Open Source Intelligence (OSINT): We gather information solely from publicly available sources, ensuring that no personal or confidential data is accessed without authorization. Our methods align with legal standards for data collection and analysis.
  • Cybersecurity Frameworks: We follow global cybersecurity frameworks that prioritize ethical hacking, privacy, and compliance with local and international laws. This ensures that we operate within legal boundaries while offering advanced cybersecurity solutions.
